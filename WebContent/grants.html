Grants

The Ardoch Development Trust receives annual income from Braes of Doune and Burnfoot wind farms. It also raises funds from donations and fundraising events to be used for community projects in the Braco and Greenloaning area.

Local groups can apply for assistance with various projects including; hire of premises, equipment and materials, one-off events or ongoing costs.

Grants can be applied for at any time; however, the committee meets at approximately six week intervals to look at applications. Early application is encouraged and we seldom assist once an event is over. Dates of ADT board meeting can be found on What's On
